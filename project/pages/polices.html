Campus Guidelines

Residential & Placement Policies

These policies help maintain discipline, fairness, cleanliness, professionalism, and a better campus environment for everyone.

Purpose

Maintain cleanliness, hygiene, room management, and fairness across campus rooms.

Room Structure

Rooms may contain approximately 7–10 students depending on occupancy.

Bed Allocation

Lower beds may be assigned based on medical or health conditions.

Cleanliness

Students are expected to maintain room cleanliness and follow cleaning schedules properly.

Eligibility

Students must complete all LMSP stages and maintain required academic scores.

Attendance

Minimum 88% attendance is required for placement eligibility.

Registration

Students must register for placements within 2 days after becoming job-ready.

Professional Behaviour

Students must maintain professionalism during interviews and placement drives.

Communication

Students should stay active on Discord and email for updates.

Integrity

Cheating or malpractice may result in suspension from placement activities.
